Critical Evaluation: Analytical vs. Numerical Approaches

Oxley Analytical Model

Strengths:

  • Computationally fast (<1 second)
  • Provides fundamental insights
  • Good for initial predictions
  • Captures trends correctly

Limitations:

  • Constant strain assumption invalid
  • 2D orthogonal only
  • Typically 20-30% error in forces
  • Cannot predict chip segmentation
