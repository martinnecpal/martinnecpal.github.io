Cutting edge preparation of cutting tools is currently one of the most modern ways to increase the tool life of cutting tools. By edge preparation in the field of cutting tools production we mean the modification of the cutting edge microgeometry of manufactured or newly sharpened cutting tools. The aim of the project is to create cutting edge rounding with new and unique edge preparation methods by plasma discharge in electrolyte, high-speed vibratory drag finishing and rotary ultrasonic machining (RUM), which have not been used for this purpose elsewhere in the world in practice and research. Cutting edge preparation is performed on prototype devices, which were originally developed at the Faculty of Materials Science and Technology STU (in the case of RUM on a machine tool with ultrasound assistance). One of the benefits will be the development of advanced edge preparation methods that can for the required cutting edge radius in an extremely short time and in the required quality.
